Dali, an amazing place in China

Dali Old Town (大理古城, Dàlǐ Gǔchéng), the original backpacker hang-out in Yunnan, was once the place to chill, with its stunning location sandwiched between mountains and Erhai Lake. Loafing here for a couple of weeks was once an essential part of the Yunnan experience. In recent years, domestic tourists have discovered Dali in a big way and the scene has changed accordingly. Instead of hippy Westerners, it's young Chinese travelers who walk around with flowers in their hair, and face-lifts of the old town precede apace.
